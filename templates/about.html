About KudumbaKart

Our Story

KudumbaKart is Kerala's premier e-commerce platform dedicated to empowering Kudumbashree units. We bridge the gap between local women entrepreneurs and customers seeking authentic Kerala products.

Our Mission

To create sustainable economic opportunities for women entrepreneurs in Kerala by providing them with a digital platform to showcase and sell their products while ensuring customers receive high-quality, authentic local goods.

What Makes Us Different

  • Direct support to Kudumbashree units
  • Quality-assured local products
  • Transparent pricing
  • Secure platform
  • Community-driven approach

Quick Facts

  • Supporting numerous Kudumbashree units
  • Located in Kerala, India
  • Products from all districts
